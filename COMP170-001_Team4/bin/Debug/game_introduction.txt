Welcome to Escape The Room. 
This is a challenge unlike any other, a true test of your intuition.
Be alert, be careful, and make smart choices.
To win this game, you must escape the room alive. 
(But watch out, the game is full of surprises)
You have three HitPoints, and certain things can harm you. 
Each thing that harms you will take 1 HP from you.

Follow the prompted commands>>:
enter: look around, examine (item), pick up (item), use (item), and 
use (item) on (item), to navigate through the game
Type: "help" for assistance with commands
Type everything in lower-case!

Type "begin" to start the game!
